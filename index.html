About

The Artist

Suresh Raja Iyer is a Chennai-born, Dallas-based music artist and founder of Suresh Raja Musicals, LLC. His music weaves together a rich classical heritage with a contemporary global sensibility.

A proud grandson of Mridangam Vidwan Dr. T.K. Murthy, Suresh carries forward a deep musical lineage — while carving his own distinct voice across genres. His songs span soulful originals to genre-crossing productions that resonate with listeners worldwide.

Beyond music, Suresh holds a doctoral degree in atmospheric chemistry — a testament to his lifelong curiosity and drive to explore the unknown, whether in science or song.

DAWganize

DAW session organizer for Ableton Live, Logic Pro & Pro Tools

Coming Soon

DAWganize is a native macOS app that brings order to the chaos of DAW sessions — the kind of tool a working producer or engineer actually needs. It parses session files from Ableton Live, Logic Pro, and Pro Tools, intelligently groups tracks by category, detects fake stereo audio, strips silence, auto-routes to aux buses, and handles multi-format stem exports. One tool. Three DAWs. Zero manual cleanup.

AI Instrument Recognition FFT spectral analysis classifies audio tracks automatically — no manual labelling needed.
Smart Track Grouping Categorizes Drums, Bass, Harmonic, Melody, Vocals & FX with a drag-drop editor and color palette support.
Stereo-to-Mono Detection Scans audio files for fake stereo (≥0.98 L/R correlation) and converts them in one click — cleaner mix, half the file size.
Stem Export Engine Export MIDI and audio stems with full control over channels, sample rate (44.1–96 kHz), bit depth, and format (WAV/AIFF/FLAC).
Tauri v2 Rust Python FFT / DSP macOS
