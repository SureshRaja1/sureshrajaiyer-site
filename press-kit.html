Suresh Raja Iyer

Singer · Songwriter · Composer · Dallas, TX

Suresh Raja Iyer is a Chennai-born, Dallas-based independent music artist and founder of Suresh Raja Musicals, LLC. A proud grandson of Mridangam Vidwan Dr. T.K. Murthy, Suresh brings a deep classical heritage to contemporary multi-genre original compositions.

His music spans Hindi, Tamil, and cross-genre productions — collaborating with acclaimed artists including Nikhita Gandhi and Abhay Jodhpurkar. By day, he holds a doctoral degree in atmospheric chemistry and works as a senior engineer.
